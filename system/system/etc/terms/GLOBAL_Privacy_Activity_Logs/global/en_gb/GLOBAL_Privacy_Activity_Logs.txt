2. What information does LGE obtain?
LGE will collect the following information:
① Mobile device status: Model, software version, IMEI, serial number, IMSI, power status, battery status, CPU/RAM/internal storage use, average use time, hardware self-diagnosis result (Wi-Fi, BT, proximity sensor, illumination sensor, fingerprint sensor, etc.)
② Network status: Service status (call, data), signal strength, station information, call frequency/time
③ Application information: List of installed and deleted apps, app version, recently executed apps, app use frequency/statistics 
④ Mobile phone connection status: Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, GPS, ear jack, battery charger, USB, and SD Card connection statuses
⑤ Error information: Frozen screen, unintended power-offs, disconnected calls, application errors (fatal, exception)

3. How will LGE use the information it obtains?
We use the information obtained from you for various purposes, including:

- performing customer services and managing the service history 
- sending push messages

We may combine the information obtained from you -- including personal information -- with information that you provide us by other means, for purposes consistent with this Privacy Policy.
