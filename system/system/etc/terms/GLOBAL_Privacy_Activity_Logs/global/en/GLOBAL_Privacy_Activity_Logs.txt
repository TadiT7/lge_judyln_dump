2. What information does LGE obtain?
LGE will collect the following information:
① Mobile device status: Model, software version, IMEI, serial number, IMSI, power status, battery status, CPU/RAM/internal storage usage, average usage time, hardware self-diagnosis result (Wi-Fi, BT, proximity sensor, illumination sensor, fingerprint sensor, etc.)
② Network status: Service status (call, data), signal strength, station information, call frequency/time
③ Application information: The list of installed and deleted apps, app version, recently executed apps, app usage frequency/statistics 
④ Mobile phone connection status: Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, GPS, ear-jack, battery charger, USB, and SD Card connection statuses
⑤ Error information: Frozen screen, unintended power-offs, disconnected calls, application errors (fatal, exception)

3. How will LGE use the information it obtains?

- To perform customer services and manage service history

- To analyze service/logs/device history for improved quality and customer satisfaction

We may combine information obtained from you, including personal information, with information that you provide to us by other means, for the purposes consistent with this Privacy Policy.
