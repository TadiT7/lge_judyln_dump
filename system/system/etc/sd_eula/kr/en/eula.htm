10. Technical and Administrative Protection Measures for Protection of Personal Information

We secure personal information safety through the following technical and administrative measures to prevent loss, theft, falsification or damage to your personal information:

¨ç    Technical Protective Measures

A.     A security device is adopted to safely transfer your personal information via network by using an encryption algorithm.

B.     To prevent external invasions such as hacking, each server controls unauthorized access from the outside and also has other technical systems for ensuring system security. We are fully committed to maintaining the security of personal information.

C.     Any information that identifies and certifies you will be encrypted prior to storage so as to prevent decoding.

D.     The personal information handler will print personal information on paper or copy it to portable storage media such as diskette or CD only with prior authorization from the chief privacy officer (CPO). The same rule applies to the reprinting or reproduction of printed materials.

¨è    Administrative Protective Measures

A.     We limit the persons who can access your personal information to the minimum.

B.     If the personal information handler changes for example due to transfer or retirement, his or her access to the personal information handling system will be changed or canceled without delay. C.     The personal information processing system and PC used by the staff responsible for handling personal information shall be set so that personal information may not be accessed by or disclosed to unauthorized persons via Internet homepages, P2P or other file-sharing setup.

D.     In collecting, using and discarding personal information, we shall record any reason for collection according to the recommended standards under applicable laws (e.g., Act on Promotion of Information and Communication Network Utilization and Information Protection) and prescribe the internal policies and process, if applicable.

E.     Every employee handling personal information shall receive in-house training on new security technology and privacy laws on a regular basis.

F.      Upon employment, every employee shall sign a Confidentiality Agreement to prevent potential leak or disclosure of personal information. Likewise, we will prepare an internal procedure for monitoring the current implementation of the privacy policy as well as employees' compliance with the said policy.

G.     The handover of the tasks for the handling of personal information is now carefully implemented under tight security, with us providing a definitive scope of the employee's responsibility in case of incidents relating to personal information security after the termination of their employment.

H.     We do not store personal information together with general data, but keep them separately on different servers.

I.       We designate the computing room and data archive as special protection zones, so access to such zones is strictly controlled.

J.      Note, however, that we shall not be held liable for damages resulting from the unexpected leak of personal information due to your carelessness or reasons other than our intentional or gross negligence.
