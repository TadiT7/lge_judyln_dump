Collection and Use of Personal Information

 

We need your help to improve the performance and accuracy of "Smart Doctor". If you agree, your "Smart Doctor" usage history will be automatically transferred to us.

 

To prevent errors of the apps and improve their functions, information on the use of applications on your mobile device will be recorded in your mobile device.

We collect the relevant personal information by using a wireless network if your mobile device is connected to the Wi-Fi network only when you agree to collection of your personal information. The collected information does not contain any information that can be used to identify the users.

 

1. Information Collected

The collected information is used only for the purpose of improving the "Smart Doctor" service and the quality of LG product.

The information collected by us includes the following:

–       Model name

–       Network status

–       Mobile device (hardware) status: Battery status, CPU/RAM/internal storage usage, self-diagnosis result of hardware, etc.

–       Error information: Frozen screens, abnormal power off, call disconnection, application error, etc.

–       Average phone usage duration

–       Application usage statistics

–       "Smart Doctor" usage count

–       Whether you changed the settings according to the "Smart Doctor" diagnosis results or its recommendations as well as whether you read the detailed explanation provided by "Smart Doctor"

 

2. Collection Method

Automatic collection during the execution or use of "the Service¡± (once a week)

 

3. Purpose of Collection

To improve the Service and the product quality

 

4. Collection Period:

We shall, in principle, destroy the collected personal information after the purpose for which it was collected was fulfilled. The personal information shall be stored for one year from the time that you agree to the collection and use of the personal information.

However, we may keep this personal information for a certain period of time, if necessary, in accordance with the relevant laws and regulations.

If you do not want us to collect your personal information, you may cancel the agreement at any time.
